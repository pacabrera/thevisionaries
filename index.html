CABRERA

Having the greatest quality of being passionate, caring, and generosity, I came up with a mission that composed of love and humanity. Looking to our situation today, there are some individuals that are neglected by the world. They have been deprived from their rights to live and been deprived to a healthy and safe environment. As per my personal life mission, what I can do is to show my care by being a part of the body of Christ.

A Hand, Mouth and Ears – part of the body that would best represent my personal life mission. Lending my hands to everyone; hands that can give a secure and clean environment for everyone. Using my mouth to address the problems to be solved; being the voice of the cries of our citizens and our land; and lending my Ears to people unheard. That is the way where I can share my care and generosity; this is the way where I can show what I am passionate about. By this mission, I am sure that everyone will live in peace, will gain their happiness, and will experience a home that is clean, healthy, and safe to live in.
