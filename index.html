PROGRESS REPORT

KEY TAKE-AWAYS (March to April 2021)

In the journey of our advocacy, tree-planting at home, we have learned to be more aware of the importance and benefits of the plants that we are taking care off. Various ways of protecting and maintaining the plants are learned, engagements to different people are improved with the use of Social media Platform and learning to appreciate God given hand that helped us to grow our plants that can contribute to protecting and adding to the beauty of our environment.

On our first progress report, we know less about what we can do but we have learned more on the process of doing our advocacy. Now, we are still learning to know more about the things that we can do and aiming for our goal; to have an improved nature and environment condition, having the assurance of everyone’s safety and to be able to make everyone plant trees, treasure, and appreciate God’s creation.

While doing our GTAP, we were surprised with the fact that we were able to encourage our whole household to plant on our own homes and we have reached several engagements in our Facebook Page. With our simple advocacy, we have encouraged people to plant, to learn their benefits and importance, and with that, we became a steward of our mainland.

Still, we wanted to be more aware of the current state of the environment. If trees are being cut down here in Angeles City, what more in places that are fully modernized. We are willing to know more about what we could do in fighting this issue. We believe that we could do more, only if we are educated about the topic because deforestation is also a complex topic and as of now, we only know little of the matter. Before it came down to deforestation there are a lot of factors why it came to be like that, there are protocols, things to be considered, and legal actions that are needed to be understood. We also wanted to learn what we could do in preventing deforestation from happening and what alternative solution we could offer.

GROUP REFLECTION

The visionaries aim to inspire and persuade people about the importance of having plants or gardens in our homes. One of the most important contributions of our advocacy creation has been to inspire other people to plant or give a short time about maintaining gardening and having it in their home to save our environment and protect against issues such as the worsening of climate change and we think through that, we became more aware and conscious of the environmental risks and problems that our country is facing as we continue to create our advocacy project. Before, we tend to disregard several environmental issues because we did not have any time to look into the problem. But now, especially when there were different issues of tree-cutting occurring in our areas, our Advocacy changed the way we view our environment. We became more cautious, protective, and appreciative. We became grateful for doing this because no one can stop us even with the pandemic. We can still make a move to contribute to our environment. It may be tough for some of us because at first, we have no idea yet but because our course, Theology, has given us insights and purpose on why we should continue to help our nature and take care of our God’s creation. Due to that, we were inspired to do the project for us to raise awareness to people who are unaware of the several tree cuttings in our country and how dangerous it is for us. Our ‘communication' is the key here in achieving our mission and development, which is why there will be no action if we do not interact in this team.

The advocacy project was an eye-opener for us. We believe that it has changed our perspective and our ways of thinking towards our interaction with the environment. It helped our personal growth to develop wherein we are able to change our behaviours and habits. It enabled us to become a person who wants to provide help or even a solution even if it does not have any big impact. Before, we used to only think of ourselves and wait for a miracle to happen. But because of our advocacy project, motivated us to move and act because that is how it was really supposed to be. Our old habits, like we spend most of our time on the internet, became more helpful because we used it in a good way, like, we were able to go out and find ways to plant trees, convince people and sign our petitions, and even inform other people through social media. Ever since we became aware of such environmental problems, we came to the thought that the solution would start with us. This advocacy is extremely beneficial to us. Particularly, establishing a small vegetable garden. It allowed us to feel relieved from stress. Additionally, the feeling of satisfaction that we influence other people makes us realize that we are really helping others in a small way. We gave some of our time to give back to our nature though our schedule is packed with different tasks from different courses. That is because we choose to grow from obliviousness, we grew as we grew our plants.

The process of the advocacy project took a long time to be finished and during those times, we learned how to team up with one another in order for us to present the project at its finest and also for the task to be done easily and quickly. We can say that the teamwork in our group was incredible because we are all responsible. We saw how each member contributed. Particularly in the part where we interacted with others, even if it was only through social media. We take turns when doing some needed engagement with our Facebook page for the project, we also make sure that all of us are present and participating when we would have a group meeting, and everyone is open when it comes to suggestions of ideas, we were able to connect our ideas with one another, helping each other, and we do not let one of us be left behind which we think helped a lot in doing the said project. If they are having a hard time finding a solution to a specific problem, we find ways to solve that. Especially that we have a great leader who guides us and keeps us organized and aligned. Due to this, we could say that accomplishing the advocacy was a team effort. While in the means of social participation, we make sure that we do not just interact with our group members but also with other people. Because of the advocacy, we were able to participate socially by sharing posts and posting information about our project on social media. The project not only made collaboration possible in our group but also made social participation possible. That is why our group is progressive and successful because all of us are moving and are willing to give our time and effort to the Advocacy Project. The team indeed met the expected outcomes from the advocacy project – cooperation, stewardship, and social conscience.

As a group, we think that it won’t be difficult to continue planting because some are fond of it, and some are already making planting a hobby. We would continue to take care of our nature not only because it is a requirement but because there is a will inside of us that keeps us doing this and we will continue this and apply it because we care for our environment. Before, we did not care about these advocacies, but now as we are tasked to do such, we became our own kind of leaders. We enjoyed and appreciated it especially that we are contributing to our environment. Each of us in the team learned much about stewardship and collaboration. Even though the project in our subject was already done, our learning could still be continuously applied because we were taught to have social conscience, upholding the responsibility for the problems and injustice of our society. We will always remember its importance even if the advocacy project is about to come to an end. We will apply the learnings we had in our course, Theology because God created this for us and we should treasure it, protect it, and love it. We will continue the page by sharing more information regarding planting trees so that many people will be convinced to plant more and sign more petitions to stop cutting the trees. We could say that we have become more of an environmentalist because of the different learnings from the project and it would be easy to apply this for something that has been learned for almost 5 months. Overall, the advocacy project is a success and we gained much knowledge about it. It is not only the plants who grew but us as well. We would always remember the vision and mission in our advocacy, and we think that we are continuing the purpose of the project.
